1. Introduction

Welcome to the Shotgun - Drive Safe App (“we,” “our,” or “us”). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application and services.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. By using the app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our services to you.

2.1. Personal Information

While using our App, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you (“Personal Information”). Personally identifiable information may include, but is not limited to:

  • Name: Your full name.
  • Email Address: Your email address.
  • Phone Number: Your contact number.

2.2. Driving Data

We collect driving-related data to enhance your safety and improve our services. This includes:

  • Location data including real-time GPS tracking.
  • Timestamps of driving sessions.

2.3 Distraction Data

The following data used to detect distractions is processed on the device and not stored:

  • Face data: Process face data live on device whenever a trip is ongoing to detect distractions.
  • TrueDepth data: Process Apple’s TrueDepth API information live on device whenever a trip is ongoing to detect distractions.

2.4. Usage Data

We monitor how you interact with the App to understand usage patterns and improve functionality:

  • App interactions and navigation.
  • Settings preferences.
  • Feature usage statistics.

2.5. Device Information

We gather information about the device you use to access our App:

  • Device type and model.
  • Operating system version.
  • Device settings and configurations.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our App.
  • To improve your driving safety through distraction detection.
  • To analyze driving patterns and behavior.
  • To enhance our AI detection algorithms.
  • To provide personalized safety recommendations.
  • To communicate with you, including customer support and updates.
  • To monitor and analyze usage and trends to improve your experience.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: You have given clear consent for us to process your personal data for a specific purpose.
